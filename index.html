We Live for the Journey

Wanderlux was born from a simple belief: travel should transform you. We curate experiences that don't just take you somewhere — they change the way you see the world.

Travel Responsibly

We believe the world's most beautiful places deserve to be protected. Sustainability isn't a checkbox for us — it's the foundation of every journey we create.

Carbon Offsetting

We offset 120% of all trip emissions through certified reforestation and renewable energy projects across the destinations we operate in.

Community First

85% of our guides, accommodation partners, and suppliers are locally owned businesses. We keep tourism money where it matters most.

Wildlife Protection

We partner only with ethical wildlife operators. No elephant rides, no captive animal shows. We fund anti-poaching rangers in every safari destination.

Plastic-Free Travel

All Wanderlux trips are single-use plastic free. Every traveller receives a reusable kit, and our guides carry out what they carry in on all treks.

Education Fund

1% of every booking goes to our Education Fund, which has put over 400 children of local guides and porters through school since 2016.

Annual Impact Report

We publish a full transparency report every year covering our carbon footprint, community investments, and conservation funding. Nothing to hide.

Flights & Transport

  • You can add flights, trains, or cab transfers during the booking process under the "Transport" step.
  • Flight changes or upgrades must be requested at least 72 hours before departure. Contact our support team via the Help Centre or email.
  • We partner with major carriers including IndiGo, Air India, Emirates, and Singapore Airlines for competitive fares.
  • Seat upgrades to Business or First Class can be arranged — contact us for a custom quote after booking.
  • Airport transfers are included in most packages; check your package details under "What's Included".

Accommodation

  • All accommodation listed in our packages has been personally inspected and approved by our team.
  • Special requests such as honeymoon setup, accessibility requirements, or dietary preferences can be noted during checkout under "Special Requests".
  • Early check-in and late check-out can usually be arranged — let us know at least 48 hours in advance.
  • Room upgrades (e.g. Standard to Deluxe Suite) are available and can be selected during the booking form.
  • All properties are vetted for sustainability standards — most are locally owned businesses.

Payments & Refunds

  • We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), UPI, and net banking.
  • A 25% deposit confirms your booking; full payment is due 30 days before departure.
  • Refunds are processed within 7–10 business days to the original payment method.
  • All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption. We never store full card numbers.
  • EMI options (3, 6, 12 months) are available on bookings over ₹50,000 through select banks.

Visas & Documents

  • Visa requirements vary by destination and passport. We provide a detailed checklist for every booking.
  • Our team can assist with visa application documentation but cannot apply on your behalf for most countries.
  • Always ensure your passport has at least 6 months validity beyond your return date.
  • For Bali, Thailand, and Maldives, Indian passport holders receive visa on arrival or e-visa — we guide you through the process.
  • Travel documentation (itinerary letter, hotel bookings) required for visa applications is provided automatically after booking.

Travel Insurance

  • We strongly recommend comprehensive travel insurance covering medical evacuation, trip cancellation, and baggage loss.
  • Wanderlux partners with HDFC ERGO and Bajaj Allianz for discounted travel insurance — ask our team for a quote.
  • To make a claim, contact your insurer directly with your policy number, booking reference, and supporting documents.
  • Medical emergencies abroad: call our 24/7 emergency line (+91 98765 00000) immediately — we coordinate with insurers.
  • Pre-existing conditions may affect coverage; disclose all health conditions when purchasing insurance.

Cancellations

  • Cancel 30+ days before departure: full refund minus the non-refundable deposit.
  • Cancel 15–29 days before: 50% refund of the total amount paid.
  • Cancel fewer than 15 days before: no refund (travel insurance may cover this).
  • To cancel, go to My Trips → find your booking → click "Cancel Booking". You'll receive a confirmation email.
  • If Wanderlux cancels due to force majeure (natural disaster, political unrest), a full refund is issued within 5 business days.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's exactly how we collect, use, and protect your data. Last updated: January 2026.

We collect information you provide directly (name, email, payment details, passport info for bookings) and usage data (pages visited, searches made) to improve your experience. We never sell your personal data.
